// About me

Who am I?

Hi there!

You have found the portfolio of Tycho Kerkhofs. Currently, I am a second-year game design student at CMGT at Saxion in Enschede (the Netherlands). I am very passionate about Virtual Reality and its games. Eventually I would like to create Virtual Reality games and applications. In my free time I do a lot with photography. Most of the time I will post my photos to my Instagram profile.

Before this study I already finished the MBO study Applicatie- & Mediaontwikkelaar at Summa College in Eindhoven (the Netherlands). Here I mainly worked with the languages C#, SQL, HTML, CSS and PHP. In the 3 years of this study, I also had 2 internships where I learned a lot about the professional IT industry.

On my website you can find information about my projects, my resume, a way to contact me, and a way to go to my other socials.
